Print Design

Book Cover Design Options

These book covers were designed to be examples of four different approaches to a single subject. The alternatives show the relationships between type and image; they are:

  • typographic
  • type accompanied by image
  • image accompanied by type
  • image as type

Weekly Event Posters

A series of posters done for an organization I used to direct. These were weekly programs that I planned with a team of volunteers and advertised through posters, flyers, cards, and media. The programs took many forms, mostly incorporating various forms of artistic expression, and were held in spaces we designed and decorated to fit the theme.

Magazine Insert Advertising

This project was for a mock speech-to-text product called speakIT. The ad needed to display the product and highlight a limited time promotional offer. In addition to the ad layout I created the logo and box artwork.
