About Me

I am a graphic designer proficient in print, mobile design, web design, and front-end web development.

I have experience working both in a team as an in-house designer as well as on my own, and in collaborations, as a freelancer. My experience with marketing research has given me insights into the value of utilizing research to tailor designs to meet the needs of the end-user; while my hands-on experience with code helps to expedite the flow between design and development.

My passion for design extends beyond the office; I always have new ideas brewing and personal projects that help me grow as a designer.
