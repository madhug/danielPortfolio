CogitoTree

CogitoTree is a story-writing web app I created & designed in order to easily write and visualize non-linear stories for game development. The core of the application is the node trees that keeps track of story steps and makes it easy to navigate the branching storylines. The nodes can also be used to keep track of characters, settings and other story assets.
