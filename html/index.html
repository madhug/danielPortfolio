About Me

I'm a graphic designer proficient in both print and interactive design. I'm a front-end developer, and I'm a marketer. I have experience working both in a team as an in-house designer as well as on my own and in collaborations as a freelancer. My experience with marketing research gives me insight into how best to tailor my designs to meet the needs of the end-user; while my experience with code and development helps me bridge the gap often found between design and development teams. My passion for design extends beyond the office; I always new ideas brewing and personal projects that help me grow as a designer.
